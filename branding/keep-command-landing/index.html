What is Keep Command?

Keep Command is a simple yet powerful command manager that allows you to create and manage your terminal commands in a beautifully structured way. Say goodbye to scrolling through endless terminal history or frantically searching through notes.

With Keep Command, you can organize your commands into folders, tag them for easy discovery, and execute them with a single click. Whether you're managing deployment scripts, database queries, or development workflows, Keep Command keeps everything at your fingertips.

Folder Organization

Create a hierarchical structure with folders to group related commands together.

Powerful Search

Search commands by name, tags, or the command itself. Find what you need instantly.

One-Click Copy

Copy commands to your clipboard with a single click and paste directly into your terminal.
